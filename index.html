About Our Church

We are a community of believers committed to following Jesus and serving our neighbors

Our Mission & Vision

At Beniyal Church Ministries, our mission is to make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and serve the world. We believe in the transformative power of the Gospel and seek to share God's love with everyone we encounter.

Founded in 2007, we have been serving our community for over two decades. Our church is built on the foundation of biblical teaching, authentic relationships, and compassionate service.

We welcome people from all walks of life to join us as we journey together in faith, hope, and love.
